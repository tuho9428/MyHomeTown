Discover Exciting Events in Long Khánh

Tết Festival

Celebrate the Lunar New Year with the vibrant Tết Festival in Long Khánh. Experience traditional customs, colorful parades, and festive performances during this joyous occasion.

Mid-Autumn Festival

Join in the fun at the Mid-Autumn Festival, where families gather to enjoy mooncakes, lanterns, and traditional lion dances. This lively event is perfect for all ages and showcases the rich cultural heritage of Long Khánh.

Lantern Night Market

Experience the magic of Lantern Night Market, a bustling bazaar filled with dazzling lantern displays, delicious street food, and lively entertainment. Browse through unique handmade crafts and soak up the lively atmosphere of this beloved community event.

Dragon Boat Racing

Witness the exhilarating Dragon Boat Racing competition along the Đồng Nai River. Cheer on the teams as they paddle fiercely to the beat of drums, showcasing teamwork and skill in this thrilling annual event.

Cultural Performances

Immerse yourself in the beauty of traditional Vietnamese music and dance at various cultural performances held throughout the year in Long Khánh. From graceful ao dai fashion shows to dynamic folk dances, these performances showcase the rich cultural heritage of the region.
