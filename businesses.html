Support Local Businesses in Long Khánh

Phúc Anh Coffee Shop

Indulge in freshly brewed Vietnamese coffee and delicious pastries at Phúc Anh Coffee Shop. Located in the heart of Long Khánh, this cozy café is the perfect spot to unwind and savor the flavors of Vietnam.

Hoa Sữa Restaurant

Experience authentic Vietnamese cuisine at Hoa Sữa Restaurant. From savory pho to mouthwatering spring rolls, Hoa Sữa offers a diverse menu of traditional dishes made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

Nghệ Anh Silk Store

Discover exquisite silk products at Nghệ Anh Silk Store. From elegant ao dai dresses to intricately woven scarves, Nghệ Anh showcases the finest quality silk products crafted by skilled artisans in Long Khánh.

Minh Trí Tailor Shop

Get tailored to perfection at Minh Trí Tailor Shop. Specializing in custom-made clothing for men and women, Minh Trí offers personalized service and impeccable craftsmanship to suit your style and preferences.

Thanh Hải Grocery Store

Stock up on essentials and local specialties at Thanh Hải Grocery Store. From fresh produce to household items, Thanh Hải provides a convenient shopping experience with friendly service and competitive prices.
