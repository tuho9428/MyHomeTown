Explore the Top Attractions in Long Khánh

Long Khánh Park

A serene oasis in the heart of the city, Long Khánh Park offers lush greenery, scenic walking paths, and recreational activities for visitors of all ages.

Suối Tre Waterfall

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city and journey to Suối Tre Waterfall, where you can relax amidst the natural beauty of cascading waters and lush forest surroundings.

Nguyễn Huệ Temple

Immerse yourself in the rich history and cultural heritage of Long Khánh at Nguyễn Huệ Temple. This historic temple is known for its stunning architecture and tranquil ambiance.

Bảo Vinh Pagoda

Discover the spiritual side of Long Khánh at Bảo Vinh Pagoda, a beautiful Buddhist temple known for its ornate decorations and serene atmosphere.
